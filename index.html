ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m David, a PhD candidate at the Music Technology Group, Universitat Pompeu Fabra. My work explores the intersection of music and artificial intelligence, with a focus on AI-generated music detection and generative models for music. I am particularly interested in developing robust methods for analyzing music and understanding the impact of generative AI on music creation, distribution, and media contexts.
